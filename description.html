Vault Helper for Destiny

Vault Helper - Destiny's item manager to transfer weapon or armor fast

Something unexpected happens? You need your touch of malice or black spindle? It's now one click from You!

Move item from vault or other your characters to your current one like a boss.

To have access to any of your armor or weapon, just select to whom you want give that item and tap. It doesn't matter if item is in vault or other character, it's always one tap from you.

Do you have to many item? Use filters and search to easily find what you need at the moment.

Do you have favorite item? Give star to that item, and it will be always on the top of your list.

Use label system to manage your loadouts
